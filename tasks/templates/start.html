How does it work

  1. Create a thread to start adding observations.
  2. Each observation has three elements: Situation, Interpretation and Approach, as well as Updates.
  3. As you go, add more and more observations, review old ones and come to new Approaches, as you close them.

Situation

Try to separate facts from anyone's reaction and emotions to the situation.

This is the first element of an observation to be filled in, typically moments after the situation happens.

Interpretation

Provide your interpretation of the situation. If there were any strong emotions and feelings here, write them down here, so that you'll learn why the situation made you so.

You can ask others to share their interpretations.

Filling this field might not be immediate.

Approach

What approach should you take in the future considering this situation? When you are ready to do so, write it down for you... or for your team members.

Filling this field might take some time.

Update on 20XX-XX-XX

Some observations need time to arrive at a specific approach. You can write as many updates as you need to arrive at a final resolution of the situation.

Closed on 20XX-XX-XX

Set close date on an observation that doesn't need any more input from anybody.
